Hardtail 29er

This specimen started out as a 2010 Jet Stream Breezer hardtail mountain bike from Craigslist. After a fair amount of legwork and cash spent it is a powerful rear hub motor ebike. Features include a modified gearing ratio thanks to a 52 tooth front chainring and a small 12 tooth rear freewheel for high speed pedaling, a bring headlight, horn, center stand, tons of storage space, suspension seat post, phone mount, and rear view mirror for riding with traffic. This makes the bike great for knocking any short trip out with ease.
Main Specs:
52 volt 28 amp-hour battery
750 watt hub motor




Hardtail 26 rebuild

This bike originally had a controller integrated into the hub motor but ultimately had a mechanical defect that had to be replaced, and per the direction of the warranty policy, all parts from the kit needed to be returned. Therefore, the bike was completely stripped down and rebuilt as you see in these photos.

I wanted to illustrate the chaos that ensued from this rebuild.

These new tires make for a good insurance policy against flats and give a smoother quieter ride than the old brittle rubber.

This is the result of the bike being about 95% finished. Since this photo was taken, a pedal assist sensor has been added per the request of the main user.
